Biography

Hello! I am currently an intern researcher at Media Computing Group, Microsoft Research Asia, where I had the privilege of working with Dr. Jinglu WANG and Dr. Xiao LI . Previously, I obtained my M.Phil. degree in Artificial Intelligence from The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, Guangzhou Campus, under the supervision of Dr. Yingcong Chen. My research interests are in Computer Vision, with a particular focus on 3D Vision and Large Vision Models. During my M.Phil. study, I spent a long time at SmartMore as a research intern, supervised by Dr. Nianjuan JIANG and Dr. Jiangbo LU.

Before that, I earned my Bachelor's degree from the School of Mathematcis at Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU).
