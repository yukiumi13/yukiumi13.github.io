Biography

I am currently doing a research internship at LIGHTSPEED STUDIOS, Tencent IEG, working on Generative 3D Models, where I am fortunate to work with Yuhan Wang and Jinnan Chen. I used to intern at Media Computing Group, Microsoft Research Asia, where I have the privilege of working with Dr. Jinglu Wang and Dr. Xiao Li . Previously, I obtained my M.Phil. degree in Artificial Intelligence from The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology supervised by Prof. Yingcong Chen and Prof. Dan Xu. My research interests are in Computer Vision, with a particular focus on 3D Vision and Large Vision Models. During my M.Phil. study, I spent a long time at SmartMore as a research intern, supervised by Dr. Nianjuan Jiang and Dr. Jiangbo Lu.

Before that, I earned my Bachelor's degree from the School of Mathematcis at Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU), where I was supervised by Dr. Heye Zhang and Dr. Zhifan Gao .
